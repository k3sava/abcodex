# Context engineering quantified, AEO continuity, volume versus curation

Four new cards, three pattern updates, no new operators. Two convergences drove the day: a quantified context engineering gap from 200 surveyed GTM operators, and a continuity argument from Lily Ray that pushes back against the frame that AEO replaces SEO. Swyx adds the quality-over-volume principle the sameness pattern was missing. Anthropic Routines closes the day with a concrete boundary between cloud-hosted and local agent execution.

## Theme 1, The 21-point gap that is not about the model

Kyle Poyar and Maja Voje's Claude for GTM Pulse Report 2026, a 37-page survey of 200 GTM operators, produced one headline number: 93% of advanced Claude users have detailed company context built into their workflow as a foundation. Among task-runner users, 72% do. That 21-point gap is the subject of `ins_poyar-voje-context-engineering-gap`. It extends `pat_context-not-capability` with the first large-sample quantitative evidence the pattern has carried.

Maja Voje's `ins_maja-voje-substrate-first-content-engineering` made the substrate argument from a content angle. Aatir Abdul Rauf's `ins_context-engineering-beats-prompt-engineering` made it from a marketing workflow angle. The Poyar and Voje survey adds scale and direct comparison. When 200 operators answer the same questions, the gap stops being anecdotal. 67% of advanced users report workflows previously impossible. 55% have replaced a tool or vendor: ChatGPT, an agency, or a contractor.

> "The top GTM AI tool stack in 2026 is Claude + CRM + orchestration. These tools are not competing. They are combining."

- Kyle Poyar + Maja Voje, `ins_poyar-voje-context-engineering-gap`. 37-page survey of 200 GTM operators: context architecture is the dividing line between teams seeing real AI value and teams running one-off prompts.

## Theme 2, AEO is an extension, not a replacement

Lily Ray's keynote at Affiliate Summit West 2026 argued against the frame that AEO replaces SEO. Her claim: the skills and signals that built domain authority in traditional search are the same ones that determine AI-generated answer citations. The target surface changes from a ranked result to an inline citation. The underlying discipline does not change.

`ins_lily-ray-aeo-seo-continuity` extends `pat_aeo-triangle` by adding the continuity operating premise. The pattern already carries measurement (`ins_aeo-three-layer-presence-readiness-impact`), citation gap detection (`ins_ghost-citation-gap`), and manual-action propagation (`ins_manual-action-propagates-to-ai-surfaces`). The continuity claim belongs in the triangle as the foundational entry point: do not restart the content program. Layer AEO signals on top of work that already demonstrates domain authority.

> "The overlap with what we've been doing in the SEO space and digital marketing space before AI search existed is very, very strong."

- Lily Ray, `ins_lily-ray-aeo-seo-continuity`. Keynote at Affiliate Summit West 2026: AEO and SEO are continuous disciplines. Same underlying signals, new target surface.

## Theme 3, Volume is not the lever

Swyx's Latent.Space piece this week landed a clean principle: there can be very little relationship between effort and result when effort means AI-generated output volume. `ins_swyx-scaling-without-slop` extends `pat_differentiation-vs-sameness` by naming the fix. Brendan Hufford's four failure modes (`ins_brendan-hufford-four-content-failure-modes`) name the categories of sameness. Swyx names the counter: aggressive curation and a point of view that does not come from the training set.

AnthropicRSQUOs Routines feature (`ins_anthropic-routines-cloud-agent-tasks`) lands as infrastructure context for teams building agent workflows. Cloud-hosted scheduled agent tasks are now a first-class artifact alongside interactive sessions. The boundary: local execution for private filesystem work and sensitive data, Routines for pure-model tasks where cloud execution is safe.

- Swyx, `ins_swyx-scaling-without-slop`. Latent.Space, week of 2026-05-10: curation and a distinct point of view are the quality levers in AI content. Volume is not.
- Anthropic, `ins_anthropic-routines-cloud-agent-tasks`. Released April 2026: Routines separates cloud-hosted scheduled agent tasks from local compute, with quota-based access across plan tiers.
