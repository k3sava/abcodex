# Data foundation determines AI agent value delivery.

## Claim
To ensure AI agents deliver real value, the foundation is critical: clear, structured, and well-organized data.

## Mechanism
AI agents can only act on the data they are given; without clean, structured data, they produce vague or inaccurate outputs. Investing in data quality first makes the agent's actions precise and trustworthy.
