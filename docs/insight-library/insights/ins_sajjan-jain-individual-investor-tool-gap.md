# Individual investors lack a simple, Buffett-style tool for deep business analysis.

## Claim
I want an easy analysis of the long term business potential of a stock, its moats, competition etc. And the app needs to explain it to me like Warren Buffett explains things, in simple terms. I don't want to look at charts and candlesticks.

## Mechanism
Existing tools serve institutions or require manual prompting per stock. A product that automates fundamental analysis and explains it in plain language for retail investors with large portfolios would fill a clear gap.
