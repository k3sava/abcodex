# LinkedIn outreach with AI scoring drove 619 new customers in 7 days.

## Claim
We used LinkedIn outreach to generate 7.8 users per day with a 41% reply rate, resulting in $80,000 and 619 new customers during an AppSumo launch.

## Mechanism
The system extracts warm leads already interested in the offer, uses an AI scoring system to pre-qualify leads, and runs a specific outreach workflow with a high-reply message.
