# Even the best sales teams can't win with bad data.

## Claim
If activity is going up but conversations are going down, that's a sign your contact data is outdated or inaccurate. Every missed connection wastes time, tanks morale, and slows down your pipeline velocity.

## Mechanism
Bad data undermines every layer of the sales stack, from dialers to enrichment tools, so even the best reps can't convert effort into pipeline. The foundation of all GTM spend is data quality; if that's cracked, ROI across the entire stack takes a hit.
