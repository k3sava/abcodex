# PMM community conversations are part therapy, part brainstorming, part celebration

## Claim
These conversations are part therapy session, part brainstorming jam, part celebration. They remind me how generous, curious, and resilient this community is.

## Mechanism
Swapping stories about new roles, career twists, and navigating a tough job market creates a powerful dynamic of learning with and from each other, which re-energizes practitioners.
