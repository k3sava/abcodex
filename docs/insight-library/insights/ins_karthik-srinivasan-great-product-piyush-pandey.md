# Piyush Pandey was himself a great 'product' worth writing about

## Claim
Piyush may have sold a lot of great products, but the truth was that he himself was a great 'product' that one could write a lot about. Ogilvy's ad lavishes writing less on his craft and more about the man himself.

## Mechanism
The best tribute ads focus on the person's character and relationships, not just their professional achievements, because that's what makes strangers feel like they got to know them.
