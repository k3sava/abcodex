# A single 'Realm of Relevancy' doc drove growth from $50K to $1.5M ARR in 9 months.

## Claim
I created a 'Realm of Relevancy' document that captured every critical piece of information relevant to our product's world, and used it to align positioning, messaging, content, and sales conversations. This doc enabled our growth from five figures ARR to $1.5M ARR in 9 months and helped secure Series A funding.

## Mechanism
The doc works because it forces the team to answer seven key questions about the product's context, like why the old process is broken, what the new method is, and what outcomes to expect, then organizes those answers into a three-part story (problem, solution, outcome). This creates a single source of truth that everyone from the CTO to the CS rep can use to educate the market consistently, generating over $10M in pipeline.
