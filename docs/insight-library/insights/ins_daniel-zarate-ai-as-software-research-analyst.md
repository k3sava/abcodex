# Use AI as a software research analyst to cut through marketing fluff and get real customer data fast.

## Claim
I got tired of marketing fluff and free trials... so I asked AI which software actually works. In under 10 minutes, I had verified insights, ratings, and comparison tables, just real data from real customers.

## Mechanism
By prompting ChatGPT with a structured role (Software Research Analyst) and specific criteria (ease of use, support, integrations, pricing, features) plus instructions to pull from verified review sources (G2, Capterra, etc.), you get a synthesized, objective comparison that would otherwise take hours of manual research.
