# The signals that earn blue-link rankings are the same ones that earn AI-generated answer citations, making AEO an extension of SEO discipline, not a replacement.

## Claim
The transition from search engine rankings to AI-generated answers does not require replacing SEO discipline. Technical SEO, content quality signals, and domain authority still determine which sources get surfaced, whether in a blue-link result or an AI-generated citation. The target surface changes. The underlying work does not.

## Mechanism
AI systems trained on web data privilege the same quality signals that search engines use to rank pages: crawlability, topical authority, clear document structure, and evidence of original expertise. The output format changes from a ranked list to an inline citation, but the selection mechanism operates on the same substrate. Operators who have built genuine domain authority and clear site architecture do not need to restart. They add AEO-specific layers (structured answer blocks, FAQ schema, passage-level answer clarity) on top of existing work.

## Conditions
Holds when: existing SEO investment has focused on genuine topical depth, crawlability, and original expertise rather than keyword density or backlink volume alone.
Fails when: prior SEO investment concentrated in technical manipulation or link schemes with little underlying content depth.

## Evidence
Keynote at Affiliate Summit West 2026. Lily Ray is VP of SEO at Amsive and one of the practitioners cited most frequently in both traditional SEO and AI search literature.

> "The overlap with what we've been doing in the SEO space and digital marketing space before AI search existed is very, very strong."

## Signals
- Existing high-quality SEO content appears in AI-generated answers without modification
- Pages that rank well in traditional search also receive LLM citations
- Technical SEO audits surface the same crawlability gaps that block AI crawler access
- Teams that skipped a full AEO rebuild still see citation volume from mature SEO content

## Counter-evidence
Some AEO-specific signals require incremental work beyond standard SEO: concise answer blocks, structured data markup, and passage-level formatting tuned for extraction. Teams focused entirely on long-form prose without structured answer layers may find their citation rates lower than their ranking positions predict. The continuity claim holds for the foundation, not for every tactic.
