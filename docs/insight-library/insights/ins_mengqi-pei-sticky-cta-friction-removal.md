# Sticky CTAs remove friction and boost conversion by 28%.

## Claim
Making the CTA sticky and changing it from transparent to solid increased signups by 28% in 15 minutes.

## Mechanism
Users who want to convert often face friction from poor UX, like having to scroll back up to find the signup button. A sticky, visible CTA removes that obstacle, letting intent turn into action without extra effort.
