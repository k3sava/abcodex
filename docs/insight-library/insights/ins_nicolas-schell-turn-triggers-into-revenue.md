# Most teams chase wrong triggers and send generic emails that kill reply rates.

## Claim
Everyone talks about triggers, but very few know how to actually turn them into revenue. Most teams chase the wrong ones, send generic 'Congrats on funding' emails, and wonder why reply rates stay low.

## Mechanism
Effective trigger-based campaigns require categorizing signals into eight types, avoiding common pitfalls like using surface-level events, and building workflows that match the signal depth to personalized outreach, which drives higher engagement.
