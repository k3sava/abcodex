# LinkedIn Ads attribution is a spreadsheet nightmare that AI can solve

## Claim
For years, as a LinkedIn Ads consultant, proving the real impact of my ads on pipeline and revenue has been pain. Not creative pain. Spreadsheet pain.

## Mechanism
The operator argues that traditional LinkedIn Ads reporting forces consultants to jump between Campaign Manager, export CSVs, and use ChatGPT, yet still struggle to answer basic questions about account engagement and revenue influence. Zena (an AI analyst) eliminates this by answering performance questions in seconds without dashboards or manual data wrangling.
