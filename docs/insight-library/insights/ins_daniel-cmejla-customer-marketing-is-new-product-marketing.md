# Customer Marketing is the new Product Marketing because harnessing customer sentiment reveals the truth of how to market.

## Claim
Customer Marketing is the new Product Marketing anyway. Because harnessing customer sentiment is the best way to discover the truth of how to market your product anyway.

## Mechanism
By closely engaging with customers and their sentiment, marketers uncover authentic insights that directly inform product positioning and messaging, making traditional top-down product marketing less effective.
