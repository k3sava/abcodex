# Simon Sinek

## Bio
Simon Sinek is the most widely recognized advocate for purpose-driven brand and organizational strategy, having popularized the idea that companies that articulate and lead with their "Why" (their purpose, cause, or belief) consistently outperform those that lead with "What" (their products) or "How" (their processes). His 2009 TED Talk, the third most-watched in history, established the Golden Circle framework as a near-universal reference point for brand strategists, startup founders, and leadership consultants.

## Operating themes
- **Operating thesis:** People don't buy what you do; they buy why you do it. And what you do simply proves what you believe. The goal is not to do business with everybody who needs what you have. The goal is to do business with people who believe what you believe. Leaders who start with why inspire action rather than manipulate it.
