# Ben Tossell

## Bio
Ben Tossell's system of thought is deeply pragmatic: he builds businesses around capabilities he personally enjoys using, structures revenue to avoid activities he dislikes (like managing advertisers), and treats AI as an accelerant for the parts of business-building that used to require teams. His trajectory from Makerpad (no-code education, sold to Zapier) to Ben's Bites (AI education, 7-figure revenue in under a year) demonstrates a repeatable playbook: identify an emerging technology wave, become the trusted curator and educator, build audience through consistent daily content, then monetize through education rather than advertising.

## Operating themes
- **Operating thesis:** AI is the co-pilot, not the autopilot -- the path to a profitable AI business is teaching others to use tools effectively, building in public, and structuring your business around what you love doing while automating what you hate.
