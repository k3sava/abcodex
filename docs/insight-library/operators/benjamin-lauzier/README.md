# Benjamin Lauzier

## Bio
Benjamin Lauzier. Built Lyft's driver supply to reach 1% of US workers; later VP Product at Thumbtack. Vocal advocate for marketplace liquidity discipline (pick the hard side first), market-health metrics over liquidity metrics, and peer mentorship as a supply-scaling mechanism.

## Operating themes
- **Liquidity before dynamics.** Pre-PMF, fill the harder side first.
- **Market health proxies.** Drive on the leading indicators that predict fill rate.
- **Peer mentorship.** Beats HR overhead for supply quality.
- **Manage via guardrails, not control.** Selective managed moves, not universal command.
