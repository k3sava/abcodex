# Matt Shumer

## Bio
Matt Shumer's perspective is grounded in six years of building AI products at the frontier, and his defining essay "Something Big Is Happening" (80M+ views) captures a practitioner's account of the moment AI capabilities crossed from "helpful tool" to "does my job better than I do." His core observation: he describes what he wants built in plain English, walks away for four hours, and returns to find the finished product -- not a rough draft requiring correction, but the completed work, tested by the AI itself, iterated until it meets the AI's own quality standards.

## Operating themes
- **Operating thesis:** We have crossed the threshold where AI demonstrates something indistinguishable from judgment and taste -- the practical implication is that technical work is being commoditized at a pace most people cannot comprehend, and the winners will be those who understand what to build, not how to build it.
