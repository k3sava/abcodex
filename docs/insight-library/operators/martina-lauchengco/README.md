# Martina Lauchengco

## Bio
Martina Lauchengco is the most authoritative voice on product marketing as a strategic discipline within the technology industry, having codified the function through SVPG's LOVED framework after nearly three decades of executive-level practice at Microsoft, Netscape, and multiple venture-backed companies. Her central intellectual contribution is the Four Fundamentals of Product Marketing -- Ambassador, Strategist, Storyteller, Evangelist -- which provides both a definition of what product marketing is and a diagnostic for what most companies get wrong.

## Operating themes
- **Operating thesis:** Product marketing is the critical connector between product and market; it operates through four fundamentals -- Ambassador, Strategist, Storyteller, Evangelist -- and must be treated as a strategic function that triangulates on product-market fit alongside product management, not a support function that produces launch assets.
