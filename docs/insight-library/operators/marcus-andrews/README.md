# Marcus Andrews

## Bio
Marcus Andrews is the practitioner who formalized narrative design as a product marketing discipline, adapting principles from video game storytelling into a structured framework for SaaS companies. His intellectual contribution is the argument that product positioning -- while necessary -- is insufficient for market differentiation in crowded categories. Positioning tells customers where your product fits; a narrative tells them why the world is changing and why that change demands a new approach.

## Operating themes
- **Operating thesis:** The best product marketing creates a narrative so compelling that it becomes the lens through which customers see their own problems; narrative design is a repeatable, structured process borrowed from game design, not a creative lightning strike.
