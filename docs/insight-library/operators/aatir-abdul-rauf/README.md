## Bio
Aatir Abdul Rauf. VP of Marketing at vFairs. Writes the Behind Product Lines newsletter on building and marketing products in lockstep. ## Operating themes

- **Positioning ≠ Messaging ≠ Copy**, three layers, three different decision rights; positioning is a business identity decision, not a marketing artifact.
