# Steph Smith

## Bio
Steph Smith's contribution to content strategy is the application of systems thinking and engineering discipline to what is traditionally treated as a creative endeavor. Her book Doing Content Right is less a manifesto than an operating manual: it covers every aspect of building a content business from topic selection through distribution, SEO, monetization, and scaling, with the systematic rigor of a product engineering playbook. Her central thesis is that content creation is a compounding asset that rewards consistency, distribution, and strategic channel selection over creative inspiration alone.

## Operating themes
- **Operating thesis:** Content is a compounding asset that rewards systematic thinking; the key to standing out is creating content that is either genuinely new or measurably better than everything else, AND distributing it better than the competition, and the explore-and-exploit method is how you discover which channels actually work.
