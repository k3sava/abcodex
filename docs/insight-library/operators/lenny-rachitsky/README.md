# Lenny Rachitsky

## Bio
Lenny Rachitsky's central contribution to AI product thinking is reframing the product manager's role from specification-writer to orchestrator of AI agents. His system of thought begins with the observation that AI is not shrinking the PM role but raising the bar: the tactical work (writing PRDs, analyzing data, creating mockups) is increasingly delegatable, which means the remaining human work -- judgment, taste, customer empathy, and cross-functional alignment -- becomes more valuable, not less.

## Operating themes
- **Operating thesis:** The best product managers in an AI world become orchestrators who define what good looks like, delegate execution to AI agents, and maintain velocity through rapid prototyping rather than specification bloat.
- **Ai Product Strategy**
- **Shipping Fast**
- **Community Led Growth**
