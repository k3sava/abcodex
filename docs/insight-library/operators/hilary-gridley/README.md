# Hilary Gridley

## Bio
Hilary Gridley writes about AI adoption from inside a product team, what actually happens when working PMs and ICs try to integrate AI tooling into a real workflow under real time pressure. Her best-known framing, the "Couch-to-5K for AI" 30-day ladder, is operationally precise about why most adoption attempts fail at the start, not at the deep end. Her diagnosis: the bottleneck is the daily 10-minute commitment under time scarcity, not the difficulty of advanced techniques.

## Operating themes
- **Operating thesis:** AI fluency is built like cardio, short daily reps over 30 days, not weekend deep-dives.
- **Adoption design**, the block is the start, not the depth.
- **Habit-shaped tooling**, practices that fit a busy operator's day actually compound.
