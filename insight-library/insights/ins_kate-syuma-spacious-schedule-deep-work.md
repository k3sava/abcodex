# Max 90 minutes of calls per day; the rest is deep work.

## Claim
I keep my schedule intentionally spacious. Max 90 minutes of calls per day. The rest is deep work.

## Mechanism
Protecting large blocks of uninterrupted time allows for high-quality creative output (course materials, client frameworks) rather than being fragmented by meetings.
