# Positioning is a strategic decision, not a democratic vote.

## Claim
Don't workshop positioning with the whole company. The CEO decides, everyone else aligns.

## Mechanism
Consensus positioning leads to watered-down strategy. Positioning must be based on evidence—who actually buys and why—and committed to for at least 6-12 months to settle in the market.
