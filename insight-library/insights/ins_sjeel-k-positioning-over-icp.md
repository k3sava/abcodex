# Clear ICP isn't enough if your positioning doesn't pull its weight

## Claim
You can have the clearest ICP in the world and still struggle to hit your growth targets because your positioning isn't pulling its weight.

## Mechanism
Founders get positioning wrong because they're too close to their product, ending up with homepages that sound like everyone else, talk features not outcomes, and leave prospects unsure why they should care or why now.
