# If you use Claude to write and it asks for your samples, are you Claude?

## Claim
If you use Claude to write, and Claude asks you for samples of your writing to build a brand voice skill, are you Claude?

## Mechanism
The paradox highlights that relying on AI to write erodes your own voice, so when the AI asks for your writing samples, you have none to give, you've become the AI.
