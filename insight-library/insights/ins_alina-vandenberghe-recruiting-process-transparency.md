# Communication is the leverage skill that turns founder-CEO into co-CEO

## Claim

After 12 months as acting CMO at Chili Piper with no formal marketing training, the biggest learning was a step-change in communication skill, going from a geek who only understood code and UX to someone writing books and articles. Communication is what unlocks the move from co-founder to co-CEO of real impact.

## Mechanism

Marketing forces a builder to translate product truth into outside-in language. Repeating that translation across landing pages, decks, and articles compounds into clearer thinking, which scales authority inside the company because every team starts trusting your framing of the problem.

## Conditions

Holds for technical founders willing to write publicly and own the narrative. Fails when communication work is delegated entirely to a CMO without the founder doing reps themselves.
