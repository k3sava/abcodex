# The fastest path to traction is giving people something they can use today, not a massive product.

## Claim
You don't need to start with a massive product. You just need to start with something that's specific enough for people to try immediately. The quickest way to get traction is to give people something they can use today.

## Mechanism
By combining a clear use case with AI that's ready right now, you can go from concept to working tool in under an hour. This rapid iteration and immediate usability creates a feedback loop that builds momentum faster than waiting for a polished, large-scale product.
