# Social media roles should be redesigned to reflect the complexity and value of the work, not just task juggling.

## Claim
After years of watching social media managers juggle strategy, content, community, reporting, and everything in between without support or recognition, we built a role that actually reflects the reality of the job.

## Mechanism
By intentionally centering creativity, strategic thinking, and community, and offering mentorship and location flexibility, the role acknowledges the true scope of social media work rather than treating it as a catch-all task list.
