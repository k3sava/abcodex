# CEO's office roles should attract those optimizing for impact, not salary.

## Claim
While the salary would be reasonable depending on the person's actual skills and experience, this is not a role where someone optimising for salary should apply.

## Mechanism
The CEO frames the role as a high-leverage position where the primary draw is exposure and impact, not immediate compensation. Candidates who prioritize salary are likely a poor fit for the role's demands and learning opportunities.
