# Agentic AI for sales is a flexible, unbound digital teammate, not a boxed-in tool.

## Claim
Instead of being boxed into a few predefined tasks, you operate with a level of flexibility + completeness that feels almost unbound. You do research, prioritize, draft, flag risks, and move work forward with minimal prompts - as if you're my digital teammate who has my back.

## Mechanism
Agentic AI systems combine multiple sales workflow steps (research, prioritization, drafting, risk flagging) into a single, minimally prompted flow, creating a sense of autonomy and completeness that traditional tools lack.
