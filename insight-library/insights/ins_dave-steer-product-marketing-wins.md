# In fast-moving markets, winning companies are clear about who they're for, what they stand for, and why they matter.

## Claim
The companies that win aren't just building great products. They're clear about who they're for, what they stand for, and why they matter. That's product marketing.

## Mechanism
Product marketing sits at the intersection of Marketing, Product, and Sales, owning the story, positioning, and proof behind the platform's power. When done well, the impact is unmistakable.
