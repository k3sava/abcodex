# Most LLM tracking tools are too basic to explain why your page is mentioned.

## Claim
Most LLM tracking tools right now are in a very very very basic stage. They can tell you if your page was mentioned, but not why.

## Mechanism
The tools lack the ability to track frequency, context, or location of mentions, and offer only surface-level suggestions like 'add more content' instead of actionable insights.
