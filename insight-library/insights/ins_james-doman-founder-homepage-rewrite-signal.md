# Rewriting the homepage repeatedly signals unclear GTM fundamentals.

## Claim
When a founder keeps rewriting the homepage, it's rarely a wording issue—it's a sign they haven't made confident calls on who this is for, which problem matters most, and what to deprioritize.

## Mechanism
Without those decisions, positioning drifts because everyone has input. The fix isn't better copy—it's revisiting ICP, positioning, and GTM strategy, then letting the bet run long enough to teach you something.
