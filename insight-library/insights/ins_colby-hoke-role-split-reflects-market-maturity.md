# Role split from open source to MLOps/AI Engineering signals market maturation

## Claim
My previous role at SAS split into two roles, with the open source work going one way and the MLOps/AI Engineering work going another.

## Mechanism
As a product category matures, the scope of PMM work becomes too broad for one person; splitting roles allows deeper specialization in distinct sub-domains (open source vs. enterprise MLOps).
