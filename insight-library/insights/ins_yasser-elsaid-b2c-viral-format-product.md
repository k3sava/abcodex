# Build B2C AI products around viral TikTok formats.

## Claim
Figure out a format that can go viral on TikTok, then build a product around it.

## Mechanism
Viral formats reduce customer acquisition cost to near zero, allowing a small team to achieve scale without paid marketing. The product itself becomes a distribution vehicle.
