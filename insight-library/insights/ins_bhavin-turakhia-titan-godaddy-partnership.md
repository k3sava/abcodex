# Embedding your product into a giant's distribution is the fastest path to scale

## Claim
Titan signed a global partnership with GoDaddy, making Titan the default business email for GoDaddy customers in developing markets. The pilot delivered a 65% lift in NPS and a 76% increase in ARPU for GoDaddy.

## Mechanism
By becoming the default option inside an existing high-traffic platform (domain registration), Titan bypasses the need to acquire customers directly. The platform's existing user base becomes your distribution, and the metrics prove that a better product lifts the platform's economics too.
