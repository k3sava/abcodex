# Signal-led AI outbound beats spray-and-pray by targeting right message to right person at right time.

## Claim
We built a signal-led, AI-powered outbound system that sent the right message to the right person at the right time, generating $1.2M pipeline in 90 days with zero outbound headcount.

## Mechanism
It works because you detect buying signals (job changes, funding, tech installs) via Clay+Clearbit, enrich with Apollo, personalize with GPT-4, then orchestrate multi-channel outreach (email, LinkedIn, retargeting, Slack) via n8n. AI handles mid-thread replies and books meetings with full context for closers.
