# Rebrands succeed when timed right, not when they look better

## Claim
Rebrands don't succeed because they look better. They succeed because they're timed right. Too early, and you're designing around assumptions. Too late, and you're correcting accumulated inconsistency.

## Mechanism
The right time is when your product has matured, market perception hasn't caught up, and growth feels slightly constrained by current positioning. That's when alignment becomes strategic.
