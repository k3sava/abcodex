# PMM teams fail when treated as content vending machines, not strategists.

## Claim
When PMM is only invited to the execution phase with no product context, buyer access, or input into narrative, the output is generic and the team burns out.

## Mechanism
PMMs need product training, ICP context, and the ability to ask questions early. Without these inputs, they default to whatever brief they're given, producing generic assets that leadership then criticizes for lacking depth.
