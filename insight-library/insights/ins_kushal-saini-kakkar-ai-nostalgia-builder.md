# AI can serve as a nostalgia builder, surfacing past work and memories.

## Claim
Discovered an amazing use case for AI today: Nostalgia builder. Was messing around with ChatGPT on the topic of sales workflows and found this blast from the recent past: 'Netflix for your sales calls' written for Wingman a couple of years ago.

## Mechanism
By prompting AI on a current topic, it can retrieve and resurface past projects and copy, creating a serendipitous trip down memory lane that reconnects you with your own work history.
