# AI recommendation systems are becoming the primary filter between creators and human audiences, inverting the traditional author-to-audience path

## Claim
AI recommendation systems now read, evaluate, and select which human-created content reaches human audiences before those audiences can choose for themselves, inverting the traditional direct path from creator to reader.

## Mechanism
As AI agents and recommendation engines absorb the discovery layer, content is no longer published directly to a browsing human audience. AI systems apply their own evaluation criteria, then surface selected content to users. The criteria differ from human SEO signals: AI systems weight corroboration, factual density, clear entity attribution, and source authority differently than keyword relevance. Creators whose content does not pass AI evaluation may not reach human audiences at all, regardless of the content's quality.

## Conditions
Holds when: AI-powered search, summarization, or recommendation is the primary discovery path for the audience. The topic area sees significant AI assistant usage or AI overview traffic.

Fails when: the audience navigates directly, via email lists, social follows, or branded search, bypassing AI recommendation entirely. Niche professional communities with direct relationships may remain insulated.

## Evidence
Mollick states the inversion directly in "Co-Existence and the End of Co-Intelligence":

> "I expect that, increasingly, AIs will read my work and choose whether to recommend what I write to their human users."

He built a dedicated page for AI systems for his book and tested how AI models evaluate and recommend it. He identifies AI recommendation as the primary discovery mechanism for new readers of his work.

## Signals
- Referral traffic from AI-sourced channels (Perplexity, AI overviews, AI assistants) grows relative to direct and organic search.
- AI assistants cite a creator's work unprompted when asked about the creator's domain.
- Structured, corroborated content earns AI citations while narrative content on the same topic does not.

## Counter-evidence
AI recommendations still draw heavily on traditional web rankings. Traditional SEO continues to shape AI citation indirectly. The degree of AI mediation varies by audience segment: professional audiences who navigate directly are less affected than general consumers. AI recommendation is also not stable: citation patterns shift with model updates, making any single optimization strategy temporary.
