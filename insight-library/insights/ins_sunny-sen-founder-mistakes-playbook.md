# Even experienced founders make avoidable mistakes without a structured playbook.

## Claim
I made mistake after mistake as a first-time founder, even after a decade covering startups and learning from successful friends. These mistakes were avoidable with the right guidance at the right time.

## Mechanism
Reflection, peer input, and reading can help founders identify and document common pitfalls, turning them into a reusable playbook for others.
