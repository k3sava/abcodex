# PMMs have a love-hate relationship with AI because no one builds tools for their workflows

## Claim
Product Marketers are having a love-hate relationship with AI right now. On one hand, we crave what it can do; on the other hand, no one is specifically focused on our workflows (per usual).

## Mechanism
The lack of AI tools tailored to PMM workflows forces individual marketers to figure out prompts and processes on their own, creating friction despite the desire for AI's capabilities.
