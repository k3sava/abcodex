# LinkedIn thought leadership is often word salad with no real point.

## Claim
If you use words like 'unlock,' 'narrative,' 'intentional,' you don't need a point. This isn't thought leadership, it's a word salad.

## Mechanism
The platform rewards buzzword-heavy, vibe-based posts that sound like growth but contain no breakthrough or insight. The author satirizes this by writing a post that is intentionally empty but framed as transformational.
