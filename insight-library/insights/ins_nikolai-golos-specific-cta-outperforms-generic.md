# Specific CTAs that promise a clear outcome convert 269% better than generic ones.

## Claim
Generic CTAs like 'Learn More' or 'Start Now' make users doubt because they don't know what happens next. A high-converting CTA solves a specific problem, uses customer language, and clearly defines the next step.

## Mechanism
Specific CTAs reduce cognitive friction by making the outcome explicit, which lowers the user's uncertainty and increases their willingness to click. The promise of a clear, valuable next step (e.g., 'Test my English level for free') aligns with the user's goal and removes the mental cost of guessing what happens after the click.
