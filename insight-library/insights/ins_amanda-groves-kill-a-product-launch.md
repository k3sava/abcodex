# A single bug can kill a product launch, so PMMs must fight for launch readiness.

## Claim
How to kill a product launch in five words or less: 'we found a new bug'.

## Mechanism
A last-minute bug derails launch momentum, erodes cross-functional confidence, and forces a delay or scrapped release. PMMs must embed quality gates and escalation paths early to prevent this.
