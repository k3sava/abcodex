# PMMs should pay it forward by offering 1:1 support during job hunts

## Claim
With a wave of PMM roles opening up, this feels like a good time to pay it forward. I'm opening up 1:1 slots to help folks navigate job hunts in Indian B2B SaaS product marketing.

Providing a safe space to stress-test your story, sharpen your resume, crack assignments, and decode interview experiences helps PMMs feel less alone and more strategic.
