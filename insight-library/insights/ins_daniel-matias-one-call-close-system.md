# The One-Call Close System eliminates the 60+ day sales cycle by combining discovery, proposal, and close into a single call.

## Claim
Most agencies use a 'Traditional Sales System' that takes 60+ days from first touch to closed deal. Instead, use the One-Call Close System: same marketing channels, but the sales meeting happens AND the SOW is presented on the same call. No discovery call, no 'let me send you a proposal,' no 30-day follow-up limbo. One call. Close or move on.

## Mechanism
Fewer steps mean fewer drop-offs. Prospects are hottest on the first call. Automation handles follow-ups and proposal generation, so the operator focuses only on closing. This tightens the system rather than chasing more leads.
