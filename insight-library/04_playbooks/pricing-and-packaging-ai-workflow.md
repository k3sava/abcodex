# Pricing and Packaging AI Workflow

## Goal
Create a repeatable pricing decision loop connecting market evidence, packaging hypotheses, and revenue outcomes.

## Workflow
1. Gather evidence: win/loss, discount patterns, usage concentration, sales objections.
2. Cluster signals with AI assistance.
3. Draft 2-3 packaging hypotheses with explicit tradeoffs.
4. Define experiment design (segment, channel, guardrails, duration).
5. Run experiment and review by cohort outcomes.
6. Promote winning structure; archive losing hypotheses with reasons.
