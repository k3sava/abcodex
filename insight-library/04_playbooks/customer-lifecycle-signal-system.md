# Customer Lifecycle Signal System

## Objective
Create a single loop connecting PMM, sales, CS, and support signals to lifecycle outcomes.

## Inputs
- Sales call objections
- Onboarding drop-off events
- Success health signals
- Support ticket themes
- Community discussion clusters

## Outputs
- Narrative updates
- Onboarding and docs revisions
- Enablement refreshes
- Expansion proof kits

## Cadence
- Weekly: signal triage and tactical fixes
- Monthly: lifecycle cohort review
- Quarterly: narrative and packaging refactor
