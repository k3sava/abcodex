# Sales Enablement AI Workflows

## Workflow 1: Objection Intelligence Loop
1. Ingest call snippets, win/loss notes, and community objections into Commons.
2. Cluster objections with LLM assistance.
3. Convert clusters into enablement cards: objection, risk, response pattern, proof asset.
4. Deploy cards to sales and track stage-level conversion deltas.

## Workflow 2: Role-Aware Narrative Routing
1. Maintain one canonical narrative in Commons.
2. Generate role-specific variants (exec buyer, practitioner, technical evaluator).
3. Validate with live-call resonance scoring.
4. Promote high-performing variants and retire low performers.

## Workflow 3: Launch Readiness Copilot
1. Build launch brief with ICP, pain, message hierarchy, proof map.
2. Auto-generate first drafts of battlecards, email copy, and demo talk track.
3. Human review for taste, risk, and differentiation integrity.
4. Run post-launch attribution review by segment and funnel stage.

## Required instrumentation
- Asset lineage IDs
- Timestamped prediction logs
- Segment-tagged outcome tracking
- Weekly review cadence with decisions recorded
