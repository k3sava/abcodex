# Cool Vendors in AI for B2B Sales Productivity (G00835102)

## Why this is in the wiki

Gartner names "machine seller" as a category. Direct precedent for flywheel's buyer-side synthetic-audience layer + the synthetic-audience-as-pre-flight-against-real-buyers pattern. Plus three productivity levers map cleanly onto the seven-stage loop.

## Key takeaways

- **By 2027, 95% of seller workflows will begin with AI** (up from <20% in 2024). Strategic planning assumption from Gartner.
- **By 2028, 75% of RevOps tasks in workflow management, data stewardship, and analytics will be executed by agentic AI.** Pressure on RevTech adoption.
- **Three productivity levers** — simplify frontline-seller path; ensure each seller has territory opportunity; resource each channel adequately.
- **Three AI use cases** Gartner names:
  1. Capture advanced activity intelligence from frontline sellers (mobile voice AI dictating meeting analysis; converting transcripts to structured CRM)
  2. Guide frontline sales decision velocity and quality (revenue action orchestration / RAO; forward-deployed engineers)
  3. Scale high-volume or self-serve channels with **machine sellers** (human-like avatars, voice/chat/video, deterministic guardrails + generative AI)
- **Vendors named:** 1mind (machine-seller avatars), plus 3 others (full list in raw doc; partial extract).

## Quotes worth keeping

> "By 2027, 95% of seller workflows will begin with AI (whether they choose to or not), up from less than 20% in 2024." (Strategic Planning Assumption, p.2)

> "By 2028, 75% of RevOps tasks in workflow management, data stewardship, and analytics will be executed by agentic AI." (Strategic Planning Assumption, p.2)

> "Forward-deployed engineers, who are software engineers embedded with clients on an ongoing basis, play a key role in the success and value of these solutions." (on RAO market direction)

## How this should influence flywheel

- **Stage 3 (synthetic-audience pre-flight):** the "machine seller" pattern is the buyer-side analog. Our synthetic buyers ARE machine-buyers. Gartner validates the category; cite this when explaining the architecture to skeptical stakeholders.
- **Stage 5 (ship):** the "frontline seller speaks to CRM, AI parses + writes structured field notes" pattern translates to "operator runs `bet new` from a chat surface, AI parses + writes the bet packet." Direct precedent for the Slack-bot's `/bet new` modal.
- **Forward-deployed engineering** as a workflow primitive — pairs with Cat Wu's "engineers with product taste" + the principal-GTM-engineer thesis. Worth surfacing in the AI-native-team-building-thesis concept page.
