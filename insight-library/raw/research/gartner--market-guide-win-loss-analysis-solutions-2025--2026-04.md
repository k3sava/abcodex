# Market Guide for Win/Loss Analysis Solutions

Rahim Kaba, Dan Tolan, David Yockelson. W/L analysis minimizes seller-data bias and surfaces purchase-decision drivers.

## Key takeaways

- 65% of CMOs/PMMs using or planning W/L solutions — +16% over 2 years.
- Seller-submitted data is intrinsically biased; third-party support institutionalizes the practice.
- Revenue + retention leaders own buyer.

## Quotes worth keeping

> "Sixty-five percent of CMOs and product marketers are currently using or plan to use win/loss analysis solutions, marking a 16% increase from two years ago." (p.1)

> "Revenue and retention leaders are often skeptical of the quality and validity of seller-submitted win/loss data."
