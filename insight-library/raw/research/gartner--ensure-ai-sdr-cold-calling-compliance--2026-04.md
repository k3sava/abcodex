# Ensure AI SDR Cold-Calling Compliance Across Global Markets

Igor Marchal + Michele Buckley on regulatory compliance for AI-driven cold outreach. Agentic AI now scales global B2B cold calling, but privacy regimes are jurisdiction-specific and fines are heavy. CEOs must verify per-country compliance for the prospect's country of residence (not the seller's).

## Key takeaways

- AI cold calling is no longer experimental — qualify, respond, follow-up, reroute to live agent all in real time.
- Compliance scope is the prospect's residence, not the seller's HQ.
- Failure-mode is regulatory fines, not just brand damage.
- Sits inside Gartner's "Power Go-to-Market Strategies With AI Innovation" initiative.

## Quotes worth keeping

> "AI cold calling is no longer experimental. Many tools are now available to engage with, qualify, respond to, follow up on the intent of, and even reroute prospects to a live agent, all in real time." (p.1)

> "Failure to comply with local laws applying to the residence of your target audience can result in heavy fines."
