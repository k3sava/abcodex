# Innovation Insight: Rethinking Battle Cards in the Age of AI

Rahim Kaba et al. on transforming static battle cards into dynamic, in-workflow competitive intel. Three AI-driven approaches PMM leaders can use to keep sellers competitively prepared.

## Key takeaways

- Traditional battle cards in content repos go stale fast — competitive risk for sellers.
- AI takes over synthesis of competitive + market intel; PMMs shift to curation + verification.
- Three approaches (full detail in PDF): in-CRM AI surfacing, conversational competitive assistant, automated battle-card generation from market signals.
- Battle cards become a workflow primitive, not a Notion page.

## Quotes worth keeping

> "AI can transform static battle cards into dynamic tools that provide real-time competitive insights directly within seller workflows." (p.1)

> "Traditional competitive battle cards, often stored in digital content repositories, quickly become obsolete in dynamic markets." (p.1)
