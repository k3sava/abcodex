# Market Guide for B2B Message Testing Solutions

Rahim Kaba, Jess Walker, David Yockelson. Emerging category: solutions that test B2B messaging effectiveness before content/campaign activation.

## Key takeaways

- "Sameness" perception is the top differentiation barrier — buyers see comparable features across products.
- PMM leaders should test differentiators with external audiences before campaign launch.
- Tools span software + services for message testing.
- Boost engagement + conversion is the explicit promise.

## Quotes worth keeping

> "Buyer skepticism and the perception of 'sameness' will limit differentiation efforts if product marketers do not test their differentiators with external audiences." (p.1)
