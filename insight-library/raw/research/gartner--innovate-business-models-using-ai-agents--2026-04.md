# Innovate Business Models Using AI Agents

Daniel Sun, Jorge Lopez, Mordecai on using AI agents not just to automate but to redesign business models.

## Key takeaways

- By 2028: 33% of enterprise software apps will include agentic AI (up from <1% in 2024).
- AI agent "machine customers" will replace 20% of interactions at human-readable digital storefronts.
- 15%+ of day-to-day work decisions will be made autonomously through agentic AI by 2028 (zero in 2024).
- Business model innovation, not just process automation, is the strategic frame.
