# Tech CEOs: Maximize B2B Sales With Marketing Automation

Julian Poulter, Amy Jenkins. Marketing automation in B2B is complex; right processes + org structure are critical. Top practices for tech CEOs.

## Key takeaways

- Often-overlooked foundation: data itself + a data strategy. Tools fail without it.
- Lead workflow brand-impression → closed-won is the heart of the system.
- Process + org structure decisions are upstream of automation tooling.

## Quotes worth keeping

> "The often-overlooked critical foundation to using any of it effectively is the data itself and having a data strategy around it."

> "The lead workflow from brand impression to closed-won business is the heart of the system."
