# Top Trend in 2026: Voice AI Enhances CX With New Use Cases

Manoj Bhatia. Voice AI = subset of conversational AI; product leaders are turning voice into a dynamic CX channel via advanced transcription, translation, interactive capabilities.

## Key takeaways

- By 2028: 75% of CAI offerings will support bidirectional multimodal UI beyond text + voice (vs. <10% in 2024).
- Voice AI unlocks humanlike conversation simulation, advanced ASR, deeper engagement.
- CSP product leaders own the opportunity.
- Strategy + Risks initiative.

## Quotes worth keeping

> "Gartner predicts that by 2028, 75% of conversational AI (CAI) offerings will have a bidirectional multimodal user interface (UI) support beyond text and voice, up from less than 10% in 2024." (p.1)
