# LLMs Are the New OS. AI Agents Are the New Apps. And SaaS Just Got Demoted to Infrastructure

5-min Mark Petty essay. Thesis: every SaaS product that depends on a UI is about to become back-end infrastructure. Claude, ChatGPT, Gemini are competing to become the surface for all enterprise workflows — same as Windows for desktop, iOS/Android for mobile.

## Key takeaways

- LLM platform = the new OS layer.
- Domain-specific agents = the new apps.
- MCP registries = the new app stores.
- Anthropic and OpenAI are building operating systems, not chat products.
- SaaS that depends on a UI is being demoted to backend.

## Quotes worth keeping

> "Every SaaS product that depends on a user interface is about to become back-end infrastructure."

> "Claude, ChatGPT, and Gemini are competing to become the surface through which all enterprise workflows are conducted. The same way Windows became the surface for desktop computing."

> "The LLM platform is the new OS. Domain-specific agents are the new apps. MCP registries are the new app stores."

> "Anthropic and OpenAI are building operating systems."
