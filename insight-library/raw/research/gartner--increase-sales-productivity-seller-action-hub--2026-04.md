# Increase Sales Productivity With an AI-Powered Seller Action Hub

Dan Gottlieb et al. on the new "system of action" tier emerging above seller workflows. CSOs must redesign sales tech to make AI the productivity layer, not another tab.

## Key takeaways

- "Systems of action" — new class of AI-powered sales tech that consolidates frontline workflows.
- Frontline seller systems overload + AI advancement create the opening.
- CSOs need a sales-tech vision; sales-tech leaders need an architecture.
- Productivity frontier is achievable, not aspirational.
