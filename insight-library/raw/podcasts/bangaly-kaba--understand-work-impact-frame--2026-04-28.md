# Bangaly Kaba — Growth PM philosophy: understand work

## Load-bearing insights

1. **Understand work precedes execution.** "First you have to really understand from first principles what is actually going on... understand, identify, execute." Not "pull data to justify a plan" (identify, justify, execute), but "what's really happening?" Data informs understanding, not the other way around.

2. **Impact = environment + skills.** "Impact is only achievable by looking at two sets of variables... related to the environment... related to your skills." Where are you structurally hindered? Where are your skills lacking? What do you control?

3. **Adjacent user is the growth inflection point.** Move the target user one notch further (marginal user). They're easier to acquire than greenfield.

4. **Change framework: vision + skills + incentives + resources + action plan.** All five required. "You need all of those to have change." Missing incentives kills even great ideas.

## Why this matters

Bangaly's philosophy: understand-first, not plan-first. Don't lock the roadmap; lock the understanding, iterate the execution.
