# Excerpt (from miniu digest 2026-04-27, 2026-04-28, 2026-04-30)

Aleyda Solis published a 3-layer framework for AI search measurement:

- **Presence** — prompt coverage, recommendation rate, linked-citation rate, comparative win rate, representation accuracy.
- **Readiness** — 10 brand traits that diagnose why the visibility looks the way it does (llms.txt presence, JSON-LD blocks, schema.org @types, FAQ structure, paragraph length, first-paragraph entity density, canonical tag, etc.).
- **Business Impact** — kept in four separate confidence buckets to stop modeled numbers from bleeding into observed.

Load-bearing line:
> "Measured AI referral traffic is the floor, not the ceiling, of AI's contribution."

Companion piece on global AI search (https://www.aleydasolis.com/en/ai-search/global-ai-search-strategy/) maps the same framework across ten markets and warns:
> "Your global competitor set is not your AI search competitor set in each market."

Earlier piece (https://www.aleydasolis.com/en/search-engine-optimization/google-march-core-update-2026/) — March 2026 core update analysis (March 26 to April 11 window). Aggregator and quick-answer utility sites dropped, destination brands and specialist sites gained. Operating rule:
> "Crawlability shapes everything."
