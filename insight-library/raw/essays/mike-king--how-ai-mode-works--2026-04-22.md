# Excerpt (from miniu digest 2026-04-28, 2026-04-29, 2026-04-30)

Mike King reframes optimization from "rank for queries" to "be retrieved at the passage level":

> "Answers are generated, not linked."

Mechanism: chunkers split pages into passages; embedding models score each passage against likely sub-queries (query fan-out); the synthesized answer pulls passages that survive chunking and embed cleanly. Optimizing for blue-link CTR is dead frame; the work is structuring passages.
