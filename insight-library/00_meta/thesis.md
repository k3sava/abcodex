# PMM OS Insight Library Thesis

This library exists to compound strategic and operational PMM/GTM intelligence into reusable execution.

## Core thesis
- **Commons** is the substrate: team-scoped shared knowledge, read-write boundaries, role-aware routing, and continuity across sessions and machines.
- **Flywheel** is the operating model: predicted-outcome logging, cohort attribution, buyer-side signals, and revenue-per-employee framing.
- **clearPMM** is the personalized and productized layer: career-ops for the builder and tiered deployment for PMM teams.

## What this system optimizes for
1. Make PMM work visible.
2. Reduce asymmetric attribution.
3. Convert thought leadership into artifacts, signals, and repeatable playbooks.
4. Preserve human taste/decision quality while automating routine coordination and measurement.
