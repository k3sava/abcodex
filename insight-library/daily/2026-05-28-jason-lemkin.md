Lemkin unpacks the ICONIQ 2026 GTM benchmark, 150+ B2B software companies surveyed. The headline: organizations with AI fully embedded in their GTM processes generate roughly 2x net new revenue per FTE compared to low adopters. The biggest delta is not in Sales -- it is in Post-Sales. AI CSMs are an order-of-magnitude unlock because human CSMs were chronically underused relative to their potential NRR impact. Orgs are 20-30% leaner and 9x flatter. The structural change he highlights: high performers have merged the hunter and farmer remits into a single AE role, betting that a hunter mentality across the full revenue org drives NRR better than handoffs do. For consulting engagements: the ROI case for AI-in-GTM is now benchmarked, not theoretical. For ab-codex: the Post-Sales finding deserves its own operator insight track -- it is where the compounding happens and where most PMM libraries go quiet.
