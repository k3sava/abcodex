# Cross-Leader Patterns

## Pattern 1: Canonical narrative, distributed variants
Maintain a single strategic narrative artifact, then route role-specific versions for buyers, users, and champions.

## Pattern 2: Artifact lineage as infrastructure
High-performing teams keep timestamps, owners, and evidence links for each strategic decision.

## Pattern 3: Learning velocity from prediction logs
Teams that predict outcomes before campaigns learn faster because they can score decision quality, not just outcomes.

## Pattern 4: AI discoverability requires entity authority
AEO success depends on trusted mentions/citations across multiple surfaces, not just rank positions.

## Pattern 5: Community as objection intelligence
Community interactions provide ongoing objection and language data that improves enablement and narrative fit.

## Pattern 6: Loop design beats campaign volume
Growth systems outperform one-off campaigns when reinvestment logic and leading indicators are explicit.
