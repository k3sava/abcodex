# Greg Isenberg Insights

## Insight: Start with repeatable interaction formats
- Leader: Greg Isenberg
- Domain: Community / Demand Gen
- Maturity: Applied
- Confidence: Medium

### Claim
Communities scale when interaction formats are repeatable (AMAs, teardown threads, office hours), not purely spontaneous.

### Mechanism
Design 2-3 recurring formats and instrument participation + conversion outcomes.

### Apply when
- Engagement depends on one personality.

### Don’t apply when
- Community has not yet found a core problem.

### Evidence signals to track
- Repeat participation and referral rates.

### OS mapping
- Commons: Interaction format playbook.
- Flywheel: Participation-to-pipeline cohorts.
- clearPMM: Community calendar ops.
