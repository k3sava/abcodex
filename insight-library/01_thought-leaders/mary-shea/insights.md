# Mary Shea Insights

## Insight: Core operating principle
- Leader: Mary Shea
- Domain: Sales Enablement / RevOps
- Maturity: Applied
- Confidence: Medium

### Claim
Enablement impact rises when training, content, and buyer-stage diagnostics are linked to conversion outcomes.

### Why it matters
Supports building a compounding PMM OS where artifacts, signals, and outcomes are linked.

### Mechanism
Define the principle in a structured template, connect it to one outcome metric and two leading indicators, then run weekly review loops.

### Apply when
- Teams cannot explain why performance changed.
- Execution quality is inconsistent across functions.

### Evidence signals to track
- Signal latency (time from action to interpretable feedback).
- Decision cycle time.
- Outcome lift per iteration.

### OS mapping
- Commons: canonical artifact + decision log.
- Flywheel: prediction, cohort attribution, and weekly delta review.
- clearPMM: personal and team operating routine.
