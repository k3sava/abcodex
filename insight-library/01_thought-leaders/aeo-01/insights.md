# Kevin Indig Insights

## Insight: Optimize for mention and citation share, not only blue-link rankings
- Leader: Kevin Indig
- Domain: AEO / GTM
- Maturity: Applied
- Confidence: Medium

### Claim
AI discovery requires content and entity strategies that increase model-visible mentions and citations across trusted surfaces.

### Why it matters
A PMM OS that only tracks SERP rank misses buying-journey influence happening in AI answers.

### Mechanism
Create source clusters (owned + earned), publish answer-structured pages, and measure AI-surface mention share by topic.

### Apply when
- Organic traffic is flat while brand search interest is rising.
- Buyers increasingly ask AI tools before visiting your site.

### Evidence signals to track
- Mention share by topic in AI assistants.
- Citation frequency of your assets.
- Brand query growth and direct traffic quality.

### OS mapping
- Commons: AI Visibility Ledger (queries, prompts, outputs, citations).
- Flywheel: Topic-to-pipeline attribution via cohort tracking.
- clearPMM: AEO operating checklist and weekly monitoring ritual.
