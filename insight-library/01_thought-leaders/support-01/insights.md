# Support Lifecycle Insights

## Insight: Support tickets are narrative telemetry
- Leader: support-01
- Domain: Support / PMM
- Maturity: Applied
- Confidence: Medium

### Claim
Ticket language reveals message gaps, expectation mismatches, and hidden objections.

### Mechanism
Cluster tickets by intent and map to messaging, onboarding, and docs fixes.

### Apply when
- Repetitive tickets persist despite product updates.

### Don’t apply when
- Ticket taxonomy quality is poor.

### Evidence signals to track
- Reopen rate, repeat-ticket rate, doc deflection rate.

### OS mapping
- Commons: Ticket-to-message map.
- Flywheel: Deflection + satisfaction trends.
- clearPMM: Monthly support insight brief.
