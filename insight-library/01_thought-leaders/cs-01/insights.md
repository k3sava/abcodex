## Insight: PMM should co-own onboarding narrative
- Leader: cs-01
- Domain: Customer Success / PMM
- Maturity: Applied
- Confidence: Medium

### Claim
Onboarding conversion improves when PMM owns the value-story progression from day 0 to first proof point.

### Mechanism
Define onboarding narrative milestones tied to activation events.

### Apply when
- Time-to-value is long and churn is early.

### Don’t apply when
- Product instrumentation is missing.

### Evidence signals to track
- Time-to-first-value, activation completion.
