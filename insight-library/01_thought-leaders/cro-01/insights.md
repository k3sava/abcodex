## Insight: Message-match is the first conversion lever
- Leader: cro-01
- Domain: CRO / PMM
- Maturity: Foundational
- Confidence: Medium

### Claim
Conversion improves first when ad/search intent, landing promise, and product proof align.

### Mechanism
Align acquisition promise with page narrative and proof blocks by intent segment.

### Apply when
- CTR is healthy but LP conversion is weak.

### Don’t apply when
- Traffic quality is fundamentally mis-targeted.

### Evidence signals to track
- Bounce rate by intent, LP CVR, qualified demo rate.

- clearPMM: Weekly conversion review.
