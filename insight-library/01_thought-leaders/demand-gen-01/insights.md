# Chris Walker Insights

## Insight: Measure demand gen by pipeline quality and revenue velocity
- Leader: Chris Walker
- Domain: Demand Gen / GTM
- Maturity: Foundational
- Confidence: Medium

### Claim
Marketing success should be judged by business outcomes (qualified pipeline, conversion, velocity), not raw MQL counts.

### Why it matters
This directly supports your Flywheel goal of reducing invisible work and asymmetric attribution.

### Mechanism
Define a small metric spine: sourced+influenced pipeline, stage conversion, velocity, and CAC payback by cohort.

### Apply when
- Teams optimize for form fills instead of revenue movement.
- Marketing and sales dispute attribution continuously.

### Evidence signals to track
- Pipeline per headcount.
- Win rate by acquisition path.
- Sales cycle compression after narrative and content changes.

### OS mapping
- Commons: Demand Experiment Log + asset lineage.
- Flywheel: Cohort-based attribution model.
- clearPMM: Weekly demand-gen operating review.
