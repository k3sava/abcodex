# Rosie Sherry Insights

## Insight: Build repeatable trust loops before scale loops
- Leader: Rosie Sherry
- Domain: Community Marketing
- Maturity: Applied
- Confidence: Medium

### Claim
Healthy communities scale when trust and norms are intentionally designed and reinforced.

### Why it matters
For your PMM OS, community is a buyer-signal engine only if the environment produces candid, high-quality interaction.

### Mechanism
Instrument onboarding rituals, contribution pathways, recognition systems, and feedback handoff into product/PMM.

### Apply when
- Community exists but is noisy, low-trust, or creator-dependent.

### Evidence signals to track
- Returning contributor rate.
- Time-to-first-value for new members.
- Volume of reusable objections/jobs-to-be-done insights.

### OS mapping
- Commons: Community signal capture template.
- Flywheel: Community-to-pipeline influence cohorts.
- clearPMM: Community-led narrative refinement workflow.
