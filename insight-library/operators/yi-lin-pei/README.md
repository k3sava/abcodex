# Yi Lin Pei

## Bio
Yi Lin Pei is a product marketing coach who works primarily with PMMs at startups and scale-ups. Her approach is distinctively practitioner-oriented — her frameworks come with filled-in examples (Teachable for positioning/messaging, multiple coaching clients for launches) and operational templates rather than abstract theory. She draws a clear hierarchy from positioning (where you fit in the market) to messaging (what you say) to copy (exact words) to storytelling (narrative that creates emotion), insisting that conflating these layers creates organizational confusion.

## Operating themes
- **Operating thesis:** Positioning and messaging are built bottom-up from customer interviews and value matrices, not top-down from internal assumptions — and great launches are strategic exercises grounded in market dynamics, not checklists of activities.

- **Messaging House Construction**
- **Value Matrix Development**
- **Customer Interview For Positioning**
