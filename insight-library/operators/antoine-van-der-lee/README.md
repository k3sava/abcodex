Antoine van der Lee. A decade of production iOS development made van der Lee one of the most-followed practitioners in the indie iOS community. He built SwiftLee into a weekly Swift and Xcode reference used by tens of thousands of developers, and RocketSim into a simulator enhancement tool. His writing covers Swift concurrency, SwiftUI animation, and AI-assisted development patterns, published at a consistent weekly cadence that rewards current platform knowledge.

## Operating themes
- **Weekly Swift craft** Publishes hands-on iOS tutorials covering new APIs, Swift language features, and Xcode tooling at each major Apple release cycle.
- **AI-native development** Documents how AI tools integrate with Apple's developer platform, including Agent Skills portability rules and Xcode 27's agentic improvements.
- **Indie iOS shipping discipline** Operates and grows two developer tools (SwiftLee newsletter and RocketSim) as a solo founder, applying direct experience to practical shipping advice.
