# Becky Kennedy

## Bio
Clinical psychologist and founder of Good Inside, a parenting company whose frameworks transfer cleanly into leadership. Public reference voice for repair-as-primary-relationship-strategy, MGI (Most Generous Interpretation), and the discipline of separating behaviour from identity in feedback conversations.

## Operating themes
- **Repair over perfection.** Trust is built by post-rupture repair, not by avoiding rupture.
- **Most Generous Interpretation.** The lens that produces interventions instead of grievance.
- **Separate behaviour from identity.** "This is a good person who shipped late."
- **Sturdy leadership over happy leadership.** "This is hard and I can do hard things."
