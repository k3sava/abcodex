# Jenny Wen

## Bio
Head of Design for Claude Co-work at Anthropic. Voluntarily returned to IC from a Director-of-Design seat at Figma when she "wasn't sure middle management was safe in the future." Earlier at Dropbox, Square, and Shopify. Vocal advocate for the position that the diverge-converge-research-mock-iterate design process is dead, replaced by a two-mode pattern of code-pairing plus 3–6 month direction-setting prototypes.

## Operating themes
- **The design pie chart shifted.** From 60% mocking to 30% mocking + 30% pairing + 20% code.
- **Three hiring archetypes.** Block-shape, deep specialist, craft new-grad.
- **Illegibility framework.** Watch for energy without articulation; that's the next bet.
- **AI taste will get good.** Humans hold the decision seat, not the unique-creative-judgment seat.
