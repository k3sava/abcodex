# Pieter Levels

## Bio
Pieter Levels is the most extreme practitioner of the "ship fast" philosophy in the indie hacking world, and his results validate the approach: 70+ launched products, multiple profitable businesses running simultaneously with zero employees, and Photo AI growing from zero to $132K MRR in 18 months as a solo operation. His methodology is deliberately contrarian to Silicon Valley orthodoxy: no fundraising, no co-founders, no employees, no complex tech stacks, no premature optimization. His tech stack is vanilla PHP, jQuery, and SQLite -- technologies that are decades old but allow him to ship products in days rather than months.

## Operating themes
- **Operating thesis:** Ship fast, charge from day one, use boring technology, and never hire -- a single person with AI tools can build and operate multiple profitable products simultaneously by treating every idea as a cheap experiment rather than a precious venture.

- **Shipping Fast**
