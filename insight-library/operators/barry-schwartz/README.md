Barry Schwartz. Founder of Search Engine Roundtable and longtime news editor at Search Engine Land. Primary source for Google algorithm updates, ranking changes, and search feature rollouts. Has tracked Google's evolution for over two decades. As AI search surfaces mature, Schwartz covers the formalization of citation mechanisms in Google AI Mode and AI Overviews, translating Google's rollout announcements into practitioner-readable signals for SEO and AEO work.

## Operating themes
- **Primary Google source coverage** First-mover reporting on algorithm changes, ranking updates, and AI search feature deployments.
- **Practitioner translation** Converts Google announcements and Search Liaison communications into specific, actionable signals for search practitioners.
- **Long-arc pattern tracking** Covers not just individual updates but the directional arc of Google's AI search investments over months and years.
