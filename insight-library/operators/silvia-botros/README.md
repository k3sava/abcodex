# Silvia Botros

## Bio
Senior Principal Engineer at Twilio (formerly SendGrid). Database and distributed-systems specialist who built one of the canonical public arguments for what a principal IC actually does. Operates as @dbsmasher on social and writes at blog.dbsmasher.com. Her 2020 LeadDev essay is widely cited in engineering-ladder design and was load-bearing for many post-2020 staff-engineer career frameworks.

## Operating themes
- Principal IC is a different role, not a more-senior senior.
- Multiplier behaviour, not output, is the qualifier for the role.
- All problems above a certain level are people problems.
- Career ladders need explicit competencies — implicit ladders favour bias.
