# Lily Ray

## Bio
Lily Ray is the SEO industry's most rigorous forensic analyst of Google algorithm updates, specializing in understanding why sites win or lose after core updates and what quality signals Google is actually rewarding or punishing. Her methodology involves large-scale before-and-after analysis of sites affected by each update, correlating ranking changes with specific E-E-A-T signals (named authors, author biographies, evidence of real staff, credentials, source citations) and content quality indicators (original research, real-world experience, authentic reviews versus manufactured content). This empirical approach has made her the go-to analyst for understanding each major Google update's actual impact, as opposed to speculation.

## Operating themes
- **Operating thesis:** Google's quality evaluation is converging on a single question: does this content come from genuine expertise and provide real-world value, or is it manufactured at scale to capture search traffic? Sites that do too much SEO and too little genuine value creation are the primary casualties of every major algorithm update.
