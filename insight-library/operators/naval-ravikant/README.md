# Naval Ravikant

## Bio
Naval Ravikant's intellectual contribution is a unified framework for individual wealth creation built on four interlocking variables: specific knowledge, accountability, leverage, and judgment. Unlike most business thinkers who focus on tactics, Naval operates at the level of principles and personal operating systems. His central formula is: Outcome = Specific Knowledge x Leverage x Judgment x Accountability, compounded over time. Each variable is necessary; none is sufficient alone.

## Operating themes
- **Operating thesis:** Wealth creation is the product of specific knowledge, leverage, and judgment compounding over time; the new leverage (code and media) is permissionless and makes individual output asymmetric.
