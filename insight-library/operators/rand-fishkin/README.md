# Rand Fishkin

## Bio
Rand Fishkin's intellectual trajectory from SEO pioneer (Moz) to audience research advocate (SparkToro) to zero-click marketing theorist represents a coherent evolution driven by data. His seminal 2024 zero-click search study found that 58.5% of US Google searches end without a click to any website, and of the remaining clicks, nearly 30% go to Google-owned properties (YouTube, Images, Maps). For every 1,000 US searches, only 360 clicks reach the open web.

## Operating themes
- **Operating thesis:** Two-thirds of Google searches now end without a click, platforms algorithmically punish outbound links, and attribution infrastructure is breaking; the only rational response is zero-click marketing, which means creating standalone value where audiences already are and measuring lift rather than clicks.
