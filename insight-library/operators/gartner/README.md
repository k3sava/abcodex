# Gartner

## Bio
Gartner is the dominant B2B technology research and advisory firm. Its published research is treated as a primary source in this corpus when the report has named lead authors, dated publication, and primary research methodology (CIO surveys, peer reviews, vendor evaluations). Cards under operator: Gartner cite the lead analyst(s) in operator_role. Cards default to Tier B because the methodology is rigorous but the source is institutional rather than first-hand operator experience.

## Operating themes
- Forward-curve forecasts (strategic planning assumptions) for enterprise tech.
- Vendor-landscape categorisation (Magic Quadrants, Market Guides, Cool Vendors).
- Operating-model frames for AI agents, GTM, sales productivity, and PMM craft.
