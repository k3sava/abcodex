Kenton Varda. Principal Engineer at Cloudflare and tech lead for Cloudflare Workers, the edge computing platform that runs on Cloudflare's global network. Varda has led the Workers runtime, its security model, and its developer tooling since the platform launched. He writes publicly about engineering decisions, AI adoption in software teams, and the practical gaps between what AI systems claim to do and what teams actually experience when using them at scale.

## Operating themes
- **Engineering context and intent** Varda focuses on the gap between what is observable in code and what needs to be communicated about why decisions were made.
- **Practical AI adoption** He approaches AI tools from the standpoint of a production engineering team, documenting where AI behavior degrades under real working conditions.
- **Infrastructure and runtime design** Cloudflare Workers' security and performance architecture reflects his long-running thinking on how to make distributed systems reliable under adversarial conditions.
