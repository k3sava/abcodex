# Amanda Natividad

## Bio
Amanda Natividad coined the term "zero-click content" and developed the strategic framework that distinguishes it from conventional content marketing. Her definition is precise: zero-click content delivers the complete insight, takeaway, or value directly within the platform where the audience already is, without requiring a click to a website to get the payoff. This is not a distribution tactic but a fundamental content creation philosophy. The reasoning is grounded in platform economics: Meta's spring 2025 Widely Viewed Content Report showed that 97.3% of all US post views on Facebook go to updates that do not include an external link, and 60% of Google searches end without a click.

## Operating themes
- **Operating thesis:** Zero-click content is the only reliable path forward in a digital ecosystem that is simultaneously hoarding traffic and eroding trust; platforms will crush your reach if you link out, so give away the full value on-platform and let brand awareness drive downstream business results.
