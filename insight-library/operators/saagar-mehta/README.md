# Saagar Mehta

## Bio

Saagar Mehta. Co-author with Rory Woodbridge of the pricing-in-the-age-of-AI analysis captured in the corpus: why seats-based pricing is a logical trap for an AI product whose core promise is shrinking the headcount the price is tied to.

## Operating themes
- **Pricing follows value, not seats.** When the product reduces the team, a per-seat metric prices against your own value.
