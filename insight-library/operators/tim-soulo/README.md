# Tim Soulo

## Bio
Tim Soulo is the strategic mind behind Ahrefs' content-led growth engine, which has become a case study in how a SaaS company can use SEO and content marketing to dominate its category. His core philosophy is built on a deceptively simple principle: the only goal of your content should be to promote your product, but this works only when your product genuinely solves problems and your promotion carries integrity. He operationalized this through a methodology he calls the "90/10 Content Strategy," where 90% of content is driven by keyword research targeting queries with organic search traffic potential and clear business relevance, while 10% is dedicated to thought leadership that builds authority and credibility on topics nobody is searching for yet but that offer unique insights.

## Operating themes
- **Operating thesis:** Ranking number one is not about being smarter than competitors through tricks but about being worthy of ranking number one through genuine expertise, credible ideas, and product-focused content that lets users experience your product in their imagination.
