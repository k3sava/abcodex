# Yamini Rangan

## Bio
CEO of HubSpot, succeeded Brian Halligan in 2021. Background in enterprise software (Dropbox, Workday, SAP) with a Chief Customer Officer arc that emphasized cross-functional alignment between marketing, sales, and customer success. In 2026 led HubSpot's public agent-first GTM rollout — a nine-agent flywheel covering Attract, Engage, and Delight stages.

## Operating themes
- Agents wired into a flywheel, not deployed in isolation.
- Customer support is the lowest-risk starting point for GTM AI.
- Sellers want conversational interfaces over data, not better dashboards.
- "Humans operating with higher impact" — agents free humans for deeper customer work.
