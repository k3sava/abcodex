# Seth Godin

## Bio
Seth Godin is the most prolific and influential marketing thinker of the internet era, having originated several foundational concepts that reshaped how practitioners think about attention, audience, and brand building. His intellectual contribution spans three decades and operates at the intersection of marketing philosophy, practical strategy, and cultural criticism.

## Operating themes
- **Operating thesis:** Marketing is no longer about the stuff that you make, but about the stories you tell. The goal isn't to maximize your numbers. The goal is to be missed if you stopped. People don't buy goods and services. They buy relations, stories, and magic.
- **Permission Marketing**
- **Brand Strategy Development**
- **Brand Differentiation**
- **Brand Narrative Construction**
