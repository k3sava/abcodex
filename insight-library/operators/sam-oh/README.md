# Sam Oh

## Bio
Sam Oh has become the most authoritative voice in video SEO through his dual role as both a practitioner (growing the Ahrefs YouTube channel to 500K+ subscribers) and an educator (creating Ahrefs' most comprehensive guides on video optimization). His methodology distinguishes between two fundamentally different video SEO disciplines: ranking videos within YouTube's own search and discovery system, and ranking videos in Google's search results. These require different optimization strategies because they rely on different ranking signals and serve different user intents.

## Operating themes
- **Operating thesis:** Video SEO is a distinct discipline from traditional web SEO because the ranking algorithm optimizes for engagement signals (watch time, audience retention, click-through rate) rather than link authority, making content quality and audience understanding the primary levers rather than technical optimization.
