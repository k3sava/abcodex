Nikhil Suresh. Founder and CEO of Hermit Tech, a Melbourne-based consultancy specializing in data infrastructure and recovering failed AI and software projects. Suresh writes under the handle Ludicity at ludic.mataroa.blog, where he publishes practitioner-level observations about AI adoption in large organizations. His July 2026 essay on AI mania and enterprise decision-making reached the front page of Hacker News within hours of publication.

## Operating themes
- **Practitioner skepticism of AI hype** Suresh draws on first-hand consulting experience to document the gap between claimed and actual AI productivity at enterprise scale.
- **Structural incentives that sustain inflated claims** His analyses identify coordination dynamics that prevent organizations from making honest assessments of AI value, including the prisoner's dilemma that silences AI vendors.
- **Radical honesty as consulting discipline** Hermit Tech's positioning is built on telling clients what their data infrastructure actually requires, not what vendors want them to hear.
