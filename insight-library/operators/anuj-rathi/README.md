# Anuj Rathi

## Bio
India product leader. Built core product surfaces at Swiggy (3-way marketplace) and Jupiter (financial services). Vocal advocate for full-stack PM thinking — owning outcomes across product, marketing, and ops — and for working-backwards methodology adapted to marketplace dynamics where standard OKRs break down.

## Operating themes
- **Full-stack PM owns outcomes, not features.** The PM is an influencer across users, engineers, marketing, and leadership.
- **Three divergent PR-FAQs.** Force decisions into "which" not "whether."
- **Four BB framework.** Brilliant Basics, Bread-and-Butter, Big Bets, Breaking Bad — make the bucket explicit.
- **Lazy/vain/selfish onboarding.** Design for the new user, not the product team.
