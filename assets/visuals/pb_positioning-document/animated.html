Annotated pipeline · 16 steps

The Positioning Build

01

Identify best customers

Fast close · stayed long · became advocates — that intersection is your ICP

02

Run customer interviews

5–10 structured · capture verbatim language — their words, not your paraphrase

03

Map competitive alternatives

Include "doing nothing" — it's always on the shortlist and it always wins by default

04

Identify capability attributes

Distinct from alternatives — not "better," different in a way that matters

05

Map attributes to customer value

Hard value (quantified) · emotional value (the dread avoided) — both required

06

Find the best-fit segment

The cluster that values your distinct capabilities most — not the largest addressable market
