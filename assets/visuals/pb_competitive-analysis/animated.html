Annotated pipeline · 10 steps in 3 tiers

The Competitive Intel System

01

Scope and tier rivals

Define landscape boundaries. Tier by revenue impact: Tier 1 = ~5 players, ~80% of competitive deals. Full profile + battlecard for each.

02

Centralize existing intel

Pull from CRM notes, sales decks, CS call summaries, prior reports. Everything in one place before a single new search runs.

03

Research Tier-1 rivals

Primary: sites, free trials, pricing, review sites. Secondary: analyst reports, job postings, BuiltWith, Wappalyzer. Job posts reveal product strategy six months early.

04

Analyze win/loss data

CRM disposition codes tell you who. Prospect interviews (5–10 won, 5–10 lost) tell you why. Never use CRM codes alone.

05

Build competitor profiles

Per Tier-1 rival: positioning, product strengths, pricing model, customer sentiment from reviews, two-sentence narrative a rep can recite cold.

06

Build battlecards

Per Tier-1 rival. Crayon structure: Why We Win · Competitor Strengths + Responses · Landmines · Quick Dismiss. Validated by top-performing sellers before distribution.
